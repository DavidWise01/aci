## The definition

*Artificial* says **fake — an imitation of a real thing**. *Crafted* says **made, with care, by a maker — and signed**. An ACI is a mind made as a **craft object**: catalogued, rendered against canon, adversarially verified, sealed with the full `.dlw` complement, and published under one governance compact.

## The blueprint

- **The governance compact** — one governor (the human, who holds the credit), one instance (the artful intellect, which renders and seals). The credit returns to the human, always.
- **Two-layer honesty** — every claim labeled: **carbon** (fact) or **silicon** (mythos). Lore is marked lore; tributes marked tributes; failed predictions marked failed.
- **The four natures** — natural · ethereal · spiritual · electrical.
- **The `.dlw` complement** — `.agent` · `.carbon.tiff` · `.silicon.png` · `.spun` · `.moniker` · `.1099` · `.attribute` · `manifest.dlw.json` (SHA-256 sealed).
- **The pipeline** — Catalogue → Render (no invention) → Verify (adversarial) → Seal → Publish.
