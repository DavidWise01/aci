HACI Project Validator v2.1 — Conversation Engine Patch

No syntax changes.
No new characters.
No new operators.

Language base:
- HACI v2.0 case semantics remain:
  lowercase = human
  UPPERCASE = AI
  Sentence Case = context/shared

Patch fixes:
1. Dual-edge lines are complete by default.
   Example:
   ? m previous result >
   is complete even in strict mode.

2. Body lines after dual-edge lines attach without making the event unresolved.
   Example:
   ? m previous result >
   search index
   rank context

3. Declarations do not remain open.
   Example:
   ! m memory >
   is a complete declaration event, not an open conversation.

4. Cross-file pairing is two-pass and order-independent.
   Return-only lines can appear before or after the outbound ask and still pair by resolved object.

5. Warnings/errors are deduplicated.

Conversation semantics:
- Parser emits nodes.
- Validator groups nodes into conversation events.
- Declarations are symbol events.
- Ask/observe events may be complete, unresolved, or paired with returns.
- Strict mode fails unresolved conversations.
