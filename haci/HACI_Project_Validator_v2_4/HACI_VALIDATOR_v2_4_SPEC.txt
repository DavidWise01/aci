HACI Project Validator v2.4 — Inbound Semantics Patch

No syntax changes.
No new operators.
No new HACI features.

Patch purpose:
v2.3 failed protocol-semantics torture because suffix ? was treated as completion.

Correct inbound semantics:
- suffix > = told / evidence / result / complete answer
- suffix ! = accepted / acknowledged / committed / complete commitment
- suffix ? = returned as question / still open / unresolved

Examples:
? m clarify this ?   -> unresolved in strict mode
? m clarify this >   -> complete
? m commit this !    -> complete
m needle ?           -> return-question, does not complete prior ask
m needle >           -> returned answer, may complete prior ask
